Commercial Solar

Slash Your Operating Costs.
Earn a Federal Tax Credit.

Alberta's deregulated electricity market means rate spikes hit businesses hardest. Solar gives you predictable energy costs — and Canada's 30% Clean Technology ITC makes the numbers work even faster.

30%
Federal Clean Technology ITC
Canada's largest commercial solar incentive — still open until 2033
Canadian corporations can claim a refundable 30% tax credit on the full capital cost of eligible solar, battery storage, and clean energy equipment. Unlike a deduction, this is a dollar-for-dollar reduction in taxes — and if it exceeds what you owe, the CRA refunds the difference.
Example: $255,000 Commercial System
System capital cost$255,000
Federal CT ITC (30%)−$76,500 refund
CCA Class 43.2 (Year 1 write-off)−$178,500 deduction
Annual energy savings$18,000–$28,000+
Effective net cost after ITC~$178,500
Talk to your accountant to confirm eligibility. We provide full CRA-ready documentation at project completion.

CEIP Commercial Financing
Commercial property owners in Lethbridge can access CEIP property-tax financing — the same program as residential, scaled for larger systems. Zero upfront, fixed rate, paid through your tax bill.
📈
CCA Class 43.2 — 100% Year-1 Write-off
Solar PV systems are classified under CCA Class 43.2, allowing businesses to fully expense the capital cost in Year 1 through accelerated capital cost allowance — dramatically improving cash flow in your first year.
⚡
Net Metering for Businesses
Commercial net metering lets you bank credits during peak production and draw on them year-round, reducing your Alberta pool price exposure and giving you predictable monthly energy costs.
🌿
Carbon Credits + ESG Value
Generate and sell Alberta carbon offset credits through the Emission Offset System. Solar also supports ESG reporting goals and can boost property value for commercial real estate.
📋
We Handle the Paperwork
CEIP applications, micro-gen permits, utility interconnection, and CRA-ready ITC documentation — all organized and delivered to you at project completion. Your accountant will thank us.
